## Design Guidelines:

- **Primary Color**: Orange (#FF8800)
- **Secondary Color**: Teal (#00C4AB)
- **Background**: Light gray (#F9FAFB) and white
- **Text**: Dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Rounded corners**: 12-16px
- **Font**: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Inspired by**: Linear, Stripe Dashboard
